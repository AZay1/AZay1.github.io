Our Purpose

SmartifyHealth is a new and modern startup aiming to bring you closer to terms with health and sport tech. We provide a monthly service for monitoring and analyzing your data, submitted by you to our portal. Our specialists will analyze your work out trends as well as general health statistics to deliver a set of work outs, diets and other goals directly to your smart devices. You can use any of the supported devices listed in our landing page, you can also choose to contact us through the contact form to inform us of a device you'd like us to add to our platform.

Why us?

Health is important, and qualitatively measuring it even more so. Our current competitors are tech giants which sell your data to the highest bidder, while we are a group of board certified doctors and health instructors with a passion. We provide full transparency about what happens to your data. We want to help you measure and improve your health, not sell it.

How?

It's easy to get started using our platform. First we ask you to download our mobile app which will connect to your smart devices and receive data as well as notify you of our reports and recommendations. You will need a subscription, we provide 3 plans, but do not worry, the 1st plan is what most people will need due to it's wide coverage.

Who supports us?

What to expect?

We provide a detailed report on your health in a form of a series of graphics and detailed text reports. This can be easily accessed from both your smartphone and smart watch. These reports will also build a schedule and diet plan for you.

How are we the future?

You'll be able to monitor and share your progress with your doctor and with the rest of our community. You'll also be able to get a free consultation to help you set up a fitness plan for your specific needs. We believe that your health is the most important thing you have.
